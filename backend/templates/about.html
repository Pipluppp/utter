About Utter

What is Utter?

Utter is an AI-powered voice platform that lets you clone existing voices and design entirely new ones. Generate natural-sounding speech in 10 languages.

Features

Clone Upload 10s–5min of audio to create a voice replica. Requires transcript.
Design Describe a voice in plain text—no audio needed. Great for creating new personas.
Generate Convert up to 5,000 characters of text to speech using any saved voice.

Supported Languages

English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, German, French, Russian, Portuguese, Spanish, Italian

Language auto-detection is available, or you can specify explicitly for best results.

Privacy & Ethics

Voice cloning technology should be used responsibly:

  • Only clone voices you have explicit permission to use
  • Audio files are stored securely for your use only
  • Your data is not used to train our models
